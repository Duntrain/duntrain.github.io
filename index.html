About 🔗

I am Chang Deng, an incoming PhD student in Econometrics and Statistics at the University of Chicago (UChicago) Booth School of Business. Before joining Booth, I earned Master’s degree in Computational and Applied Mathematics at UChicago, advised by Bryon Aragam. Prior to coming to UChicago, I obtained my Bachelor’s degree in Applied Mathematics at the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Bingsheng He.

  • From August 2019 to July 2020, I worked as researched assistant with Professor Yu Zhang at SUSTech Department of Computer Science. From Sep 2017 to Feb 2019, I also worked in Research Institute for Interdisciplinary Sciences (RIIS) at SHUFE.
